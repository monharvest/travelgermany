Travel Germany does not currently use affiliate links.

If we add affiliate links in future, affected articles will show a clear disclosure near the top: “Transparency note: some links in this article are affiliate links — they cost you nothing and support the site.”

Affiliate relationships will never change our editorial standards or the prices you pay.
